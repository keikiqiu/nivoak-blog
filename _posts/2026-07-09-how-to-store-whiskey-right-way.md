<p>Whiskey's three enemies: light, temperature swings, and air. Get these right and your whiskey stays at its best.</p><h2>1. No Direct Sunlight</h2><p>UV light breaks down flavor compounds. Store in a cabinet away from windows.</p><h2>2. Stable Temperature</h2><p>60-70°F is ideal. Keep away from ovens and AC vents.</p><h2>3. Minimize Air Exposure</h2><p>Store bottles upright (alcohol corrodes corks). Transfer to smaller bottle when half empty.</p><h2>Don't Refrigerate</h2><p>Fridge dulls flavors. Use <a href="https://nivoak.com/product/whisky-stones-gift-set-stainless-steel-ice-cubes/">whiskey stones</a> for a cold pour instead.</p><h2>How Long?</h2><p>Opened bottle stays good 1-2 years. Finish within 6 months if half empty.</p>
