<p>Hosting a whiskey tasting is easier than you think. Here's everything you need.</p><h2>What You'll Need</h2><ul><li>4-5 bottles, varied styles (bourbon, scotch, rye, Irish)</li><li>Small glasses (tumblers work fine)</li><li>Room temperature water</li><li>Plain crackers to cleanse the palate</li><li>Notepads and pens</li><li><a href="https://nivoak.com/shop/">Whiskey stones</a> for guests who want a chill</li></ul><h2>The Format</h2><ol><li>Start light, move to heavier whiskeys</li><li>Pour 0.5oz each — small pours are enough</li><li>Look at color, nose the aroma, taste, note the finish</li><li>Discuss after each whiskey</li><li>Vote on favorites</li><li>Revisit favorites for a full pour</li></ol><h2>Dos and Don'ts</h2><p>Do: provide water and snacks, encourage honest opinions, have a designated driver plan. Don't: pour too much, rush, or pressure anyone to finish a pour.</p>
