<p>You don't need thousands of dollars. A corner of your living room or a small cart is enough.</p><h2>Step 1: Pick a Spot</h2><p>Any flat surface works — bar cart, sideboard, bookshelf, kitchen counter. Away from sunlight and heat.</p><h2>Step 2: Essentials ($50-100)</h2><ul><li>2-4 tumblers ($15-30)</li><li><a href="https://nivoak.com/shop/">Whiskey stones</a> for no-dilution chilling ($20-35)</li><li>Pourer or stopper ($5-10)</li><li>Bar towel ($5-10)</li></ul><h2>Step 3: Starter Whiskeys (3-5 bottles)</h2><p>One bourbon, one scotch, one rye, one wild card (Japanese or Irish). Variety over quantity.</p><h2>Step 4: Add Over Time</h2><p>Decanter, tasting glass, ice mold, tasting journal — nice upgrades for later.</p>
