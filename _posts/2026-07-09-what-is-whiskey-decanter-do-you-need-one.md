<p>A whiskey decanter is a decorative glass container for storing and serving whiskey. Unlike wine, whiskey doesn't need to breathe — but a decanter adds elegance to your bar.</p><h2>What to Look For</h2><ul><li>Lead-free crystal or quality glass</li><li>Tight-fitting stopper to prevent evaporation</li><li>Wide base for stability</li><li>Easy to clean</li></ul><h2>Decanter vs Bottle</h2><p>Original bottle is fine for daily use. Decanter adds class for entertaining. Short to medium-term storage only (weeks to months).</p><h2>Caring for Your Decanter</h2><p>Clean after each use with warm water. Dry upside down. Avoid soap if possible.</p><p>Build you home bar at <a href="https://nivoak.com/shop/">Nivoak's shop</a>.</p>
