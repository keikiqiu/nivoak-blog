<p>Whiskey and food pairing isn't complicated. Match strength with strength. Here's how.</p><h2>Match Intensity</h2><p>Higher-proof bourbon goes with bold foods (grilled steak, aged cheese). Lighter Irish whiskey works with seafood and light dishes.</p><h2>Bourbon Pairings</h2><ul><li>Dark chocolate — sweetness complements bitterness</li><li>Grilled steak — char and oak are a natural match</li><li>Smoked BBQ — handles smoky, sweet, spicy</li></ul><h2>Scotch Pairings</h2><ul><li>Speyside + Salmon — honeyed notes complement fish</li><li>Islay + Blue Cheese — peat smoke and funky cheese</li><li>Highland + Roasted Nuts — sherry notes with almonds</li></ul><h2>Tips</h2><p>Serve slightly chilled with <a href="https://nivoak.com/product/whisky-stones-gift-set-stainless-steel-ice-cubes/">whiskey stones</a>. Take small sips. Don't overthink it.</p>
