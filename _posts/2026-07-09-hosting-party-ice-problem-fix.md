<h2>Problem 3: Messy Drink Stations</h2>
<p>Melting ice means wet countertops, sticky spills, and constantly refilling ice buckets. Whiskey stones are dry, clean, and easy to handle. Guests can just grab a few stones and go — no tongs, no scoops, no mess.</p>

<h2>Problem 4: Different Drink Preferences</h2>
<p>At a party, you'll have different drinkers: some want their whiskey neat, some want it cold, some are mixing cocktails. Whiskey stones work for all of them. Neat drinkers skip them. Cold-drinkers add stones. Cocktail makers use them instead of ice for no-dilution mixed drinks.</p>
