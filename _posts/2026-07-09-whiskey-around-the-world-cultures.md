<p>Every culture drinks whiskey differently. Here's a tour of global traditions.</p><h2>Scotland</h2><p>Whisky (no e) is drunk neat or with a splash of water — never ice. The water opens up the aromas. Glass: Glencairn.</p><h2>United States</h2><p>Bourbon culture is relaxed and experimental. Neat, on the rocks, or in cocktails like the Old Fashioned. Glass: Tumbler.</p><h2>Japan</h2><p>Highball culture — whiskey with soda water and ice. Precisely measured and carefully stirred. Glass: Highball glass.</p><h2>Ireland</h2><p>About hospitality.
